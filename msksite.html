    MSK

    MSK is a Museum of Fine Arts in Ghent. Togheter with another school from the Netherlands we've bin asked te question: 'how to bring people from 20 to 35 years to a museum'. With 3 other students we created a plaform on how we can connect people with art. We made a site, a game and an app. Beside those thing we had printed cards and poster with expressions from Ghent.
