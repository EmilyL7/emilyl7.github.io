    MSK

    MSK is a Museum of Fine Arts in Ghent. Together with another school from the Netherlands we've been asked to search for a solution to the following question: 'how to bring people from 20 to 35 years to this museum'. With 3 other students we created a plaform on how we can connect people with art. We made a website, a game and an app. Beside those things we printed cards and posters with typical expressions from Ghent.
